Motown. The Motor City. The D.

Detroit's engines are revved up and providing a diverse endless opportunity for recreational and cultural experiences. The Detroit Medical Center is located in Detroit's redeveloping, historic Midtown neighborhood. The DMC hospitals share the neighborhood with new and refurbished dwellings, restaurants, businesses, museums, The Detroit Symphony Orchestra, and Wayne State University Campus.

Michigan. Michigan is... umm.. awesome.

The State of Michigan and the metropolitan area offers all-season fun for the outdoor enthusiast. The great outdoors is filled with ample opportunity for hiking, biking, boating and all types of skiing to name a few.

Culture

There are many cultural activities ranging from the Detroit Institute of Arts, the Detroit Science Center, the Detroit Historical Museum, the Museum of African American History, and the Detroit Opera House. The Riverwalk, restaurants, casinos, and many theater and music venues are Downtown, all within minutes of the hospital .

Sports

Detroit is the home of the Detroit Tigers, Lions and Red Wings. The Tiger's and Lion's Stadiums are minutes from Detroit Receiving Hospital. Detroit has a rich history in sports and is definitely a "sports town". The Red Wings play at Joe Louis Arena located downtown along the waterfront. The Detroit Pistons play in the Palace of Auburn Hills, located in Auburn Hills, a suburb located north of the city.
